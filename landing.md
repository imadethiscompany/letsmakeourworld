### Proven Revenue Models (2026)
| Model | How It Works | Ideal Use‑Case | Typical ROI |
|-------|--------------|----------------|------------|
| **Pay‑per‑Call** | Charge per request (e.g., $0.001 per call) | Low‑volume, high‑value data (e.g., credit scoring) | 2‑5× cost reduction |
| **Tiered Subscription** | Fixed monthly fee for tiered usage caps | Predictable B2B SaaS APIs | 20‑30% uplift in ARR |
| **Revenue Share** | Take a % of downstream revenue generated by API data | Marketplace & fintech integrations | Aligns incentives, fast adoption |
| **Freemium + Usage‑Based** | Free tier + overage charges | Developer community APIs | 40‑60% conversion to paid |
| **Feature‑Add‑On Packs** | Sell premium endpoints or SLA guarantees | Mission‑critical services | Boost average deal size by 25% |
