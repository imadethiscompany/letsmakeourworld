# Software Monetization Models and Strategies for 2020

*Unlock the revenue potential of your software product with proven monetization frameworks.*

---

## Why Monetization Matters
In a crowded SaaS landscape, a great product isn’t enough. You need a **clear, repeatable revenue engine** that aligns with your customers’ buying behavior and maximizes lifetime value.

## 1️⃣ Subscription (Recurring) Model
- **Flat‑rate** – Simple monthly/annual fee. Ideal for B2B tools with predictable usage.
- **Tiered** – Different feature sets per price tier. Drives upsell and matches diverse budgets.
- **Usage‑based** – Pay‑as‑you‑go (e.g., per‑user, per‑transaction). Perfect for scalable platforms.

### Best Practices
- Offer a **free trial** (7‑14 days) to lower friction.
- Use **annual discounts (20‑30%)** to improve cash flow.
- Show **pricing comparison tables** that highlight the ROI of higher tiers.

## 2️⃣ One‑Time Purchase (License) Model
- **Perpetual license** – Up‑front payment, optional support contracts.
- **Feature add‑ons** – Sell extra modules or integrations.

### Best Practices
- Bundle **premium support** as an upsell.
- Provide a **30‑day money‑back guarantee** to reduce risk.

## 3️⃣ Freemium → Paid Conversion
- Core functionality free forever; advanced features behind a paywall.
- Works well for **developer tools** and **consumer apps**.

### Best Practices
- Track **activation metrics** (e.g., daily active users) to identify conversion triggers.
- Use **in‑app messaging** to showcase premium benefits.

## 4️⃣ Marketplace / Revenue Share
- List your app on platforms like **Shopify App Store**, **Salesforce AppExchange**, or **Zapier**.
- Earn a **percentage of each transaction**.

### Best Practices
- Optimize your **listing SEO** (keywords, screenshots, demo video).
- Offer **co‑marketing funds** to partners for higher visibility.

## 5️⃣ Transaction / Commission Model
- Charge a **percentage or flat fee** per transaction processed through your software (e.g., payment gateway, booking system).

### Best Practices
- Clearly display **fees per transaction** to avoid sticker shock.
- Provide **volume discounts** for high‑throughput customers.

## 6️⃣ Hybrid Approaches
Combine models: e.g., **subscription + transaction fees** for a SaaS marketplace.

### Why Hybrid Works
- Captures **steady recurring revenue** while sharing in the **growth upside** of user activity.

## Actionable Strategy Checklist
1. **Identify your target segment** – SMB vs. Enterprise.
2. **Choose a primary model** that matches buying habits.
3. **Design pricing tiers** with clear value differentiation.
4. **Implement a free trial or freemium** to lower entry barriers.
5. **Set up automated billing** (Stripe, Recurly, Paddle).
6. **Measure LTV vs. CAC** – aim for LTV/CAC > 3.
7. **Iterate quarterly** – A/B test pricing, packaging, and messaging.
