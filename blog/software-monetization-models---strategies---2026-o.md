## 1. Core Monetization Models (2026)
| Model | How It Works | Ideal For | 2026 Trend |
|-------|--------------|-----------|------------|
| **Flat‑Rate Subscription** | Fixed recurring fee (monthly/annual). | B2B SaaS with steady value. | Annual plans dominate for enterprise discounts. |
| **Usage‑Based (Pay‑As‑You‑Go)** | Bill per unit, API call, or active user. | Platforms, APIs, infrastructure. | AI‑compute pricing spikes – per‑token models. |
| **Tiered Subscription** | Multiple plans with feature caps. | Products with clear value ladders. | Hybrid tiers combine flat + usage caps. |
| **Freemium → Paid** | Free core, premium add‑ons. | Consumer‑facing apps, dev tools. | Conversion rates >5% with in‑app nudges. |
| **Outcome‑Based (Value‑Based)** | Charge a % of the business outcome (e.g., revenue uplift). | High‑impact B2B solutions. | Growing with AI ROI guarantees. |
| **Marketplace / Revenue Share** | Take a cut of transactions processed through your platform. | Marketplaces, plug‑in ecosystems. | 15‑30% standard share. |

## 2. Emerging 2026 Strategies

Integrate with complementary SaaS and share revenue on joint customer wins.

## 3. Choosing the Right Model – Quick Decision Framework
1. **Customer Purchase Power** – Enterprise vs SMB vs Consumer.
2. **Value Frequency** – Is value delivered continuously or as a one‑off outcome?
3. **Scalability Needs** – Do you need predictable cash flow?
4. **Competitive Landscape** – What are rivals charging?
5. **Data Availability** – Can you reliably meter usage?

Score each factor 1‑5, sum the scores. The highest‑scoring model is your starting point.
