## Why Most Integrations Fail to Close Deals
- **Siloed teams** – product builds what they think customers want, sales sells what they think solves a problem.
- **No data loop** – lack of feedback from closed‑won deals means you keep iterating in the dark.
- **Opportunity leakage** – features never get mapped to a buyer persona or buying stage.

## The Aligning P Methodology
1. **Map Product Features to Buying Stages** – tie each upcoming feature to a specific stage in the buyer’s journey.
2. **Create Closed‑Loop Deal Triggers** – when a prospect shows interest, auto‑create a feature‑demo task for the product team.
3. **Score Opportunities with Integration Value** – add a “Integration Fit” score to your CRM to prioritize deals.
4. **Iterate Quarterly** – use win‑loss analysis to refine the map and boost close rates.
