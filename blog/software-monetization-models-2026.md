- **Customer expectations** – Users now demand pay‑as‑you‑go, usage‑based, or outcome‑based pricing.

## Top 5 Monetisation Models for 2026

| Model | When to Use | Key Benefits | Real‑World Example |
|-------|--------------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Subscription (SaaS)** | Ongoing service, regular updates | Predictable cash flow, easy upsell | Slack, Adobe Creative Cloud |
| **Usage‑Based (Pay‑Per‑Use)** | Variable consumption, low entry barrier | Aligns cost with value, low churn | AWS Lambda, Twilio |
| **Freemium → Paid** | Acquire mass users, prove value | Large user base, viral growth | Zoom, Notion |

| **Marketplace & Revenue Share** | Platform ecosystem, third‑party extensions | Scalable ecosystem, shared risk | Shopify App Store, Atlassian Marketplace |

## 2026‑Ready Monetisation Strategy Blueprint

1. **Segment Your Audience** – Use product‑usage analytics to create **3‑5 personas** (e.g., SMB, Enterprise, DevOps, Data‑Science). 
2. **Pick a Core Model** – Start with **Subscription** for baseline revenue, then layer **Usage‑Based add‑ons** for heavy users.
3. **Design Tiered Plans** –
   - **Starter** – $9/mo, limited features, ideal for freelancers.
   - **Growth** – $49/mo, full feature set, up to 10k API calls.
   - **Enterprise** – Custom pricing, SLA, dedicated support.
4. **Add Outcome‑Based Upsell** – Offer a **Revenue‑Share** option where you take 5‑10% of the customer’s incremental profit generated by your software.
5. **Implement a Marketplace** – Open an **API‑first extension marketplace**; take a 15% revenue cut from partner apps.
6. **Continuous Pricing Experiments** – Run A/B tests on plan limits, pricing, and bundling every **30‑45 days**.

### FAQs
**Q:** *Do I need to rebuild my product to adopt usage‑based pricing?*  
**A:** Not necessarily. Most SaaS platforms can add usage tracking via existing analytics and a simple metering layer.

**Q:** *How risky is outcome‑based pricing?*  
**A:** It aligns incentives. Start with a **pilot** on a low‑risk client, set clear KPI targets, and scale.
