## Building an AI SaaS: Step‑by‑Step Blueprint
### 1. Identify a Pain Point
   - Use keyword research tools (Ahrefs, SEMrush) to find high‑search‑volume problems.
### 2. Choose the Right AI Model
   - Large Language Models (LLMs) for text‑centric products.
   - Computer Vision models for image/video analysis.
   - Time‑Series models for forecasting.
### 3. Prototype Quickly
   - Leverage **OpenAI**, **Claude**, or **Cohere** APIs.
   - Deploy on **Vercel** or **Render** for instant scaling.
### 4. Validate with Early Users
   - Offer a **free‑trial** or **beta** program.
   - Capture feedback via NPS and usage analytics.
### 5. Pricing Strategy
   - **Freemium**: Core AI features free, premium models pay‑as‑you‑go.
   - **Usage‑Based**: $0.001 per API call + subscription tier.
   - **Enterprise**: Custom contracts with SLA.
### 6. SEO & Content Marketing
   - Target long‑tail keywords: *"AI‑powered CRM SaaS"*, *"machine learning inventory management"*.
   - Publish **case studies**, **how‑to guides**, and **benchmark reports**.
   - Build **backlinks** through guest posts on tech blogs.
