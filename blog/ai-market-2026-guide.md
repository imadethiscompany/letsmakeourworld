## How Companies Monetize AI

1. **Productize AI Models** – Offer SaaS APIs (e.g., language, vision) with tiered pricing.
2. **Consulting & Integration** – High‑margin services to embed AI into existing workflows.
3. **Data Marketplace** – Curate and sell high‑quality training data.
4. **Revenue‑Sharing Partnerships** – Co‑develop solutions and split profits.
