### Why Traditional Pricing Fails
- **One‑size‑fits‑all pricing** leaves money on the table.
- **Up‑front licences** create high churn after the first year.
- **Hidden fees** erode trust and damage brand reputation.

### Proven Monetization Models (Pick What Fits Your Product)
1. **Subscription (SaaS) – Tiered Plans**
   - Core, Pro, Enterprise tiers.
   - Monthly & annual discounts.
   - Upsell add‑ons (API calls, storage, support).
2. **Freemium → Paid Conversion**
   - Free core features, premium premium.
   - In‑app nudges based on usage thresholds.
3. **Usage‑Based Billing**
   - Pay‑per‑use for compute, data, or transactions.
   - Caps & overage alerts to avoid bill shock.
4. **Marketplace/Revenue Share**
   - Integrate with platforms (e.g., Shopify, Azure Marketplace) and take a % of each sale.
5. **License + Maintenance**
   - Up‑front licence + annual support contract.
6. **Feature‑Based Add‑Ons**
   - Sell premium modules (e.g., analytics, AI engine) separately.

### Step‑by‑Step Strategy to Implement
1. **Segment Your Users** – Identify power users, casual users, and enterprise prospects.
2. **Map Value to Price** – Quantify ROI for each segment.
3. **Select Primary Model** – Choose the model that aligns with buying behavior.
4. **Design Tiered Packages** – Create 3‑4 clear plans with distinct benefits.
5. **Set Pricing Anchors** – Use a high‑priced “Premium” plan to make mid‑tier look affordable.
6. **Build Checkout Flow** – One‑click, auto‑renew, and secure payment integration.
7. **A/B Test Headlines & CTAs** – Optimize conversion rates continuously.
8. **Measure LTV & Churn** – Use cohort analysis to refine pricing.

### Benefits You’ll Gain
- **Predictable Recurring Revenue** – Smooth cash flow for growth.
- **Higher Customer Lifetime Value** – Up‑sell and cross‑sell pathways.
- **Scalable Business Model** – Revenue grows with usage, not headcount.
- **Reduced Sales Cycle** – Self‑serve pricing accelerates sign‑ups.

### FAQ
**Q:** *Will freemium hurt my brand?*  
**A:** Only if the free tier is too generous. Keep core value behind a paywall and use it to showcase product quality.

**Q:** *How do I prevent churn?*  
**A:** Offer annual discounts, proactive support, and regular feature releases.
