## A Proven PMI Framework (3‑Step Model)

1. **Strategic Alignment** – Define *what* success looks like (cost savings, revenue growth, market expansion) and *how* you’ll measure it.
2. **Operational Execution** – Translate strategy into detailed work‑streams, assign owners, and lock‑step timelines.
3. **Value Capture & Optimization** – Track realized synergies, resolve gaps, and institutionalise best‑practices.

Each step is supported by **15+ ready‑to‑use templates** (org‑chart, risk register, communication plan) that you can clone from our portal.
