## How to Stand Out
- **Portfolio First** – Host a sleek, mobile‑friendly showcase on Behance or a personal site. Include case studies with measurable results.
- **Quantify Impact** – e.g., “Boosted CTR by 42% on a $1M media spend for a fintech client.”
- **Network Locally** – Attend **Boston Marketing Mixer** events, volunteer for pro‑bono projects to get insider referrals.
- **Tailor Your Resume** – Use keywords from the job description: *media buying, programmatic, brand storytelling, ROI analysis*.
