# AI Agents: The Future of Intelligent Automation

## What Are AI Agents?
AI agents are autonomous software entities that can perceive, reason, and act on behalf of users or businesses. Unlike traditional AI models that only provide predictions, AI agents *do* things—schedule meetings, analyze data, generate content, and even run entire workflows without human intervention.

1. **Perception** – Sensors & APIs ingest data (emails, calendars, databases).
2. **Reasoning** – Large‑language models and rule‑based engines decide the next action.
3. **Action** – The agent executes tasks via integrations (Zapier, custom APIs, RPA tools).
