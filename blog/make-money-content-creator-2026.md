## 2. Build a Scalable Content Engine

1. **Identify a Niche with Low Competition & High Demand**
   - Use tools like **Ahrefs Keywords Explorer** (2026 update) to find keywords with **KD < 20** and **search volume > 5k**.
   - Example niches: *AI‑generated art tutorials*, *remote work productivity hacks*, *crypto‑gaming reviews*.
2. **Batch‑Create Content**
   - Record **5‑10 short videos** in one session using the **Blend Studio AI‑camera**.
   - Repurpose each video into a **blog post**, **Twitter thread**, and **TikTok snippet**.
3. **Automate Distribution**
   - Use **Zapier 2026** to push new YouTube videos to **LinkedIn Pulse**, **Substack**, and **Discord** automatically.
4. **Measure & Iterate**
   - Track **RPM**, **CPM**, and **ARPU** in a unified **Google Data Studio** dashboard.
