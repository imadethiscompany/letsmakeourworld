### Proven Monetization Models
| Model | How It Works | Typical SaaS Impact |
|-------|--------------|----------------------|
| **Pay‑per‑use AI calls** | Charge per API call or interaction with the agent. | Predictable incremental revenue.
| **Tiered AI features** | Bundle advanced agent capabilities into higher‑tier plans. | Drives upgrades and reduces churn.
| **Marketplace commissions** | Offer a marketplace where third‑parties sell custom AI agents and take a cut. | Expands ecosystem and creates network effects.
| **Data licensing** | Sell anonymized insights generated by agents to partners. | Opens new B2B revenue streams.
